DFS Psuedocode

 DFS(G, u) (u is the vertex where the search starts)
    Stack = []
    for each vertex i, visited[i] = false
    Stack.push(u)
    while(Stack is not empty) do:
        i = Stack.pop()
        if( visited[i] == false) then:
            visited[i] = true
            for each unvisited neighbor j of i:
                Stack.push(j)
